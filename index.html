The Seal and Serpent Society

The Seal and Serpent Society is one of Cornell University's oldest and most storied organizations. We are a community of individuals dedicated to fostering leadership, camaraderie, and personal growth. Join us during our spring rush events to learn more about our legacy and how you can become a part of it.
